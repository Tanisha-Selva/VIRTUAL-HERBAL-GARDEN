Cumin (ஜீரகம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Cuminum cyminum

TAMIL NAME: சூம் / ஜீரகம்

PARTS USED: Seeds (Dried), Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Highly effective digestive stimulant.
  • Carminative: Reduces gas, bloating, and indigestion.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and stomach irritation.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from damage.
  • Improves Metabolism: Supports weight management.
  • Anti-microbial: Fights harmful gut bacteria.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Indigestion & Gas (அஜீரணம், வாயுத்தொல்லை)

  • Cure/Relief: Boosts digestive fire & reduces bloating.
  • Application: Cumin water or roasted cumin powder after meals.

2. Acidity & Stomach Burning

  • Cure/Relief: Neutralizes excess acid & soothes the stomach.
  • Application: Boiled cumin water taken warm.

3. Digestive Infections

  • Cure/Relief: Anti-microbial action fights gut infections.
  • Application: Cumin tea or decoction.

4. Weight Loss & Metabolism Support

  • Cure/Relief: Increases fat-burning rate.
  • Application: Cumin water on an empty stomach.

5. Cold, Cough & Immunity

  • Cure/Relief: Clears mucus and supports immune strength.
  • Application: Cumin tea with ginger or honey.
