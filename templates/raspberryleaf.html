Raspberry Leaf (ராஸ்பெர்ரி இலை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Rubus idaeus

TAMIL NAME: ராஸ்பெர்ரி இலை

PARTS USED: Leaves, Tea, Extracts

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Uterine Tonic: Strengthens and supports uterine health.
  • Rich in Vitamins & Minerals: Provides iron, calcium, and vitamins.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces muscle cramps and inflammation.
  • Digestive Aid: Supports healthy digestion.
  • Pregnancy Support: Helps in easing labor and delivery.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Pregnancy & Labor Support

  • Benefit: Strengthens uterus and eases labor.
  • Application: Raspberry leaf tea daily during late pregnancy (consult physician).

2. Menstrual Cramps & PMS Relief

  • Benefit: Reduces cramps and discomfort.
  • Application: Tea or herbal infusion made from dried leaves.

3. Digestive Health

  • Benefit: Supports digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Application: Brewed leaf tea as a gentle digestive aid.

4. Anti-inflammatory & Antioxidant Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells and reduces inflammation.
  • Application: Regular consumption of leaf tea or extract.

5. General Health & Immunity

  • Benefit: Provides essential nutrients and supports overall well-being.
  • Application: Leaf infusion or powdered supplement daily.
