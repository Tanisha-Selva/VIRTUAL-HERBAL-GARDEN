Cloves (கிராம்பு)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Syzygium aromaticum

TAMIL NAME: கிராம்பு

PARTS USED: Flower buds, Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful antimicrobial and antiseptic herb.
  • Analgesic: Reduces toothache and oral pain.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Helps reduce swelling.
  • Digestive Stimulant: Improves digestion and reduces gas.
  • Expectorant: Clears phlegm and supports respiratory health.
  • Antioxidant: Protects tissues from oxidation.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Toothache & Oral Infections

  • Cure/Relief: Numbs pain and kills bacteria.
  • Application: Apply clove oil (diluted) or place a clove on the affected tooth.

2. Indigestion, Gas & Bloating

  • Cure/Relief: Stimulates digestive enzymes.
  • Application: Clove tea or adding cloves to food.

3. Cold, Cough & Throat Infection

  • Cure/Relief: Acts as an expectorant and antimicrobial.
  • Application: Clove-infused water, tea, or steam inhalation.

4. Inflammation & Joint Pain

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces swelling and pain.
  • Application: Clove oil mixed with carrier oil applied gently.

5. Boosting Immunity

  • Cure/Relief: Strong antimicrobial activity supports immune function.
  • Application: Regular intake in tea or food.
