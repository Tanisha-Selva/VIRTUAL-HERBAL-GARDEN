Rosemary (ரோஸ்மேரி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Rosmarinus officinalis

TAMIL NAME: ரோஸ்மேரி

PARTS USED: Leaves, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Memory Enhancer: Improves focus and cognitive ability.
  • Hair Growth Booster: Stimulates follicles and reduces dandruff.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Relieves swelling and joint stiffness.
  • Digestive Support: Helps in relieving gas and indigestion.
  • Antimicrobial: Fights bacteria and fungal infections.
  • Stress Relief: Calms nerves and reduces fatigue.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Memory & Concentration

  • Benefit: Boosts focus, alertness, and memory power.
  • Application: Rosemary tea or inhaling leaf steam.

2. Hair Growth & Scalp Health

  • Benefit: Reduces dandruff and increases hair thickness.
  • Application: Rosemary oil mixed with coconut oil applied to scalp.

3. Pain & Inflammation Relief

  • Benefit: Treats joint pain, muscle stiffness, and swelling.
  • Application: Warm rosemary leaves compress or tea.

4. Digestive Aid

  • Benefit: Reduces bloating, gas, and indigestion.
  • Application: Rosemary infusion before meals.

5. Stress & Fatigue Reduction

  • Benefit: Calms mind and reduces anxiety.
  • Application: Aromatherapy using rosemary leaves or oil.
