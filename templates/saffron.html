Saffron (குங்குமப்பூ)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Crocus sativus

TAMIL NAME: குங்குமப்பூ

PARTS USED: Stigmas, Threads

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Supports digestion and liver health.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and swelling.
  • Cardiovascular Support: Helps maintain healthy blood circulation.
  • Mood Enhancer: Supports mental health and reduces stress.
  • Immune Support: Boosts overall immunity.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Digestive Support

  • Benefit: Improves digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Application: Saffron infused in warm milk or water.

2. Mood & Stress Relief

  • Benefit: Enhances mood and reduces anxiety.
  • Application: Saffron tea or powdered threads with honey.

3. Liver & Detox Support

  • Benefit: Promotes liver health and detoxification.
  • Application: Small amount of saffron in herbal decoctions.

4. Cardiovascular Health

  • Benefit: Supports healthy circulation and blood pressure.
  • Application: Regular consumption of saffron infused drinks.

5. Antioxidant & Immune Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells and strengthens immunity.
  • Application: Daily use in food or beverages.
