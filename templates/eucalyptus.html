Eucalyptus (நீலகிரித்தைல மரம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Eucalyptus globulus

TAMIL NAME: நீலகிரித்தைல மரம்

PARTS USED: Leaves, Oil, Bark

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Respiratory Tonic: Clears airways and relieves congestion.
  • Anti-bacterial & Anti-viral: Useful for colds, flu, and infections.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain.
  • Antioxidant: Protects body from oxidative stress.
  • Wound Healing: Speeds recovery and prevents infections.
  • Natural Decongestant: Steam inhalation clears nasal passages.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Cold, Cough & Flu

  • Benefit: Relieves nasal congestion and cough.
  • Application: Steam inhalation or eucalyptus oil rub on chest.

2. Respiratory Infections & Asthma

  • Benefit: Opens airways and reduces inflammation.
  • Application: Eucalyptus tea or oil diffused in air.

3. Muscle Pain & Arthritis

  • Benefit: Reduces soreness and joint inflammation.
  • Application: Oil massage or warm compress with eucalyptus oil.

4. Skin Wounds & Infections

  • Benefit: Cleans wounds and prevents infections.
  • Application: Eucalyptus oil diluted with carrier oil for topical use.

5. Mental Clarity & Stress Relief

  • Benefit: Refreshes mind and improves focus.
  • Application: Diffuse eucalyptus oil in room or inhale directly.
