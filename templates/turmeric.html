Turmeric (மஞ்சள்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Curcuma longa

TAMIL NAME: மஞ்சள்

PARTS USED: Rhizome (Root), Powder, Extracts

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation in joints and tissues.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress and aging.
  • Liver Support: Enhances detoxification and liver function.
  • Digestive Aid: Improves digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Antimicrobial: Fights infections and boosts immunity.
  • Wound Healing: Promotes faster healing of cuts and skin issues.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Joint Pain & Arthritis

  • Benefit: Reduces pain, stiffness, and inflammation.
  • Application: Turmeric paste or powder with warm milk.

2. Digestive & Liver Health

  • Benefit: Improves digestion and supports liver detox.
  • Application: Turmeric water, tea, or supplements.

3. Skin & Wound Healing

  • Benefit: Reduces infections and promotes healing.
  • Application: Topical turmeric paste or combined with oils.

4. Immunity Boost

  • Benefit: Strengthens immune system naturally.
  • Application: Turmeric tea or capsules daily.

5. Anti-inflammatory & Antioxidant Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells and reduces chronic inflammation.
  • Application: Regular intake of turmeric in diet or as extract.
