Ashwagandha (அமுக்கரா)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Withania somnifera

TAMIL NAME: அமுக்கரா (Amukkara)

PARTS USED: Roots, Leaves, Seeds

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful **Rasayana herb** used for rejuvenation and longevity.
  • Nervine Tonic: Strengthens the nervous system and reduces stress.
  • Adaptogen: Helps the body adapt to physical and mental stress.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces joint pain and swelling.
  • Balances Vata Dosha: Improves sleep, calms the mind, and boosts vitality.
  • Strength Enhancer: Improves muscle mass, stamina, and energy levels.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Stress, Anxiety & Mental Fatigue

  • Cure/Relief: Calms the mind, reduces anxiety, enhances mental clarity.
  • Application: Root powder taken with warm milk at night.

2. Insomnia & Poor Sleep

  • Cure/Relief: Promotes deep sleep and reduces nighttime stress.
  • Application: Amukkara choornam mixed with ghee before bedtime.

3. Muscle Weakness & Low Energy

  • Cure/Relief: Boosts energy, muscle strength, and stamina.
  • Application: Ashwagandha powder taken daily with honey or milk.

4. Joint Pain & Arthritis

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces inflammation and improves mobility.
  • Application: Root extract consumed; leaf paste applied on joints.

5. Low Immunity & Frequent Illness

  • Cure/Relief: Strengthens the immune system and fights infections.
  • Application: Daily consumption of Ashwagandha tablet or kashayam.

AS DESCRIBED IN SIDDHA LITERATURE

** Theraiyar Yemaga Venba / Agathiyar Gunavagadam **
