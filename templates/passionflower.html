Passionflower (கொடித்தோடை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Passiflora incarnata

TAMIL NAME: கொடித்தோடை

PARTS USED: Leaves, Flowers, Stems

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Calming & Sedative: Reduces anxiety, nervousness, and insomnia.
  • Antispasmodic: Relieves muscle cramps and nervous tension.
  • Digestive Aid: Supports healthy digestion.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces mild inflammation.
  • Heart Support: Helps maintain healthy blood pressure and heart rate.
  • Relaxant: Promotes restful sleep and mental calmness.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Anxiety & Nervousness

  • Benefit: Reduces stress and nervous tension.
  • Application: Passionflower tea or tincture before bedtime.

2. Insomnia & Sleep Disorders

  • Benefit: Promotes restful and deep sleep.
  • Application: Leaf infusion or standardized extract.

3. Muscle Spasms & Cramps

  • Benefit: Relieves tension and cramps naturally.
  • Application: Tea, decoction, or tincture.

4. Mild Digestive Complaints

  • Benefit: Supports smooth digestion.
  • Application: Passionflower tea with meals.

5. Heart & Blood Pressure Support

  • Benefit: Helps maintain a calm heart rate and normal blood pressure.
  • Application: Leaf extract or infusion daily.
