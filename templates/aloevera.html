Aloe Vera (கற்றாழை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Aloe barbadensis Miller

TAMIL NAME: கற்றாழை (Katrazhai)

PARTS USED: Leaf gel, Leaf latex (juice), Whole leaf

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Katrazhai is widely used in Siddha medicine for cooling, healing, rejuvenation, and digestive benefits.
  • Sitagaram: Natural coolant that reduces excess body heat (Pitta).
  • Vranaropana: Excellent wound healer and skin repair agent.
  • Kabha Pitham Neekki: Balances both Kapha and Pitta doshas.
  • Laxative: Acts as a gentle purgative to relieve constipation.
  • Rasayana: Rejuvenates tissues and enhances overall vitality.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Skin Healing (Burns, Rashes, Wounds)

  • Cure/Relief: Heals burns quickly, reduces irritation, and regenerates skin.
  • Application: Fresh gel applied directly on the skin reduces inflammation and accelerates healing.

2. Digestive Problems & Constipation

  • Cure/Relief: Acts as a mild laxative and soothes gastric irritation.
  • Application: Small quantity of aloe latex or juice taken in the morning improves bowel movement.

3. Diabetes Support

  • Cure/Relief: Helps regulate blood sugar levels naturally.
  • Application: Aloe gel or juice is consumed daily under supervision of a Siddha practitioner.

4. Hair & Scalp Health

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces dandruff, strengthens roots, and prevents hair fall.
  • Application: Aloe gel applied to scalp nourishes follicles and prevents dryness.

5. Ulcer & Gastritis

  • Cure/Relief: Soothes stomach lining and reduces acid inflammation.
  • Application: Aloe juice taken in small amounts before food helps relieve pain and burning sensation.
