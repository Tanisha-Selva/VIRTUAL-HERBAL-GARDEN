Amla (நெல்லிக்காய்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Phyllanthus emblica

TAMIL NAME: நெல்லிக்காய் (Nellikai)

PARTS USED: Fruit, Seed, Leaves

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Amla is considered one of the most powerful rejuvenating herbs in Siddha medicine.
  • Sitagaram: Natural coolant reducing excess body heat.
  • Rasayana: Enhances immunity, longevity, and vitality.
  • Tridosha Hara: Balances Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
  • Chakra Suddhi: Purifies blood and improves skin glow.
  • Deepana & Pachana: Improves digestion and strengthens the stomach.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Immunity Booster & General Tonic

  • Cure/Relief: Improves immunity, energy, and resistance to illness.
  • Application: Amla juice or fresh fruit is taken daily to strengthen the system.

2. Skin Glow & Anti-Aging

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces pigmentation, slows aging, improves complexion.
  • Application: Regular intake of amla or external application of paste helps rejuvenate skin.

3. Hair Growth & Scalp Health

  • Cure/Relief: Prevents hair fall, reduces dandruff, strengthens follicles.
  • Application: Amla oil or paste applied on scalp stimulates growth and prevents greying.

4. Digestive Problems

  • Cure/Relief: Treats acidity, indigestion, constipation.
  • Application: Amla juice or powder taken before food improves digestion.

5. Diabetes Support

  • Cure/Relief: Helps lower blood sugar and supports pancreatic health.
  • Application: Dried amla powder consumed daily under medical supervision.

AS DESCRIBED IN SIDDHA LITERATURE
