Evening Primrose Oil (மாலை ப்ரிம்ரோஸ் எண்ணெய்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Oenothera biennis

TAMIL NAME: மாலை ப்ரிம்ரோஸ்

PARTS USED: Seeds, Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Hormone Balancing: Supports estrogen and progesterone regulation.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Helps reduce joint and skin inflammation.
  • Skin Health: Improves eczema, acne, and dryness.
  • Menstrual Relief: Reduces PMS and menstrual cramps.
  • Heart Health: Supports healthy blood vessels.
  • Rich in GLA: Contains gamma-linolenic acid for cellular function.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. PMS & Menstrual Disorders

  • Benefit: Reduces cramps, bloating, and mood swings.
  • Application: Daily capsule or oil supplement in the morning.

2. Skin Conditions (Eczema, Acne)

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and improves skin texture.
  • Application: Topical oil or oral capsules daily.

3. Joint Pain & Arthritis

  • Benefit: Eases swelling and stiffness in joints.
  • Application: Oral capsule or massage with diluted oil.

4. Hormonal Imbalance

  • Benefit: Supports endocrine function and fertility.
  • Application: Taken as capsule daily or added to foods.

5. Cardiovascular Health

  • Benefit: Maintains healthy blood circulation.
  • Application: Regular intake of evening primrose oil in diet.
