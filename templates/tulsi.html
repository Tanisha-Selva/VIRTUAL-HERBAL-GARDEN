Holy Basil (Tulsi) - (துளசி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Ocimum sanctum / Ocimum tenuiflorum

TAMIL NAME: துளசி

PARTS USED: Leaves, Stems, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful adaptogen supporting stress relief and mental clarity.
  • Immunity Booster: Strengthens body’s defense against infections.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and joint discomfort.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from free radicals.
  • Respiratory Support: Relieves cough, cold, and congestion.
  • Supports Heart Health: Regulates blood pressure and cholesterol.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Stress & Mental Health

  • Benefit: Reduces anxiety and improves mental clarity.
  • Application: Tulsi tea or fresh leaf consumption daily.

2. Immunity & Infections

  • Benefit: Enhances immune response and fights common colds.
  • Application: Tulsi leaf extract or tea.

3. Respiratory Problems

  • Benefit: Clears congestion and reduces inflammation in airways.
  • Application: Inhalation of steam with Tulsi leaves or herbal tea.

4. Heart & Blood Pressure

  • Benefit: Supports healthy blood pressure and cardiovascular function.
  • Application: Daily consumption of Tulsi juice or tea.

5. Anti-inflammatory & Antioxidant Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells, reduces joint pain and inflammation.
  • Application: Tulsi leaves as powder, decoction, or in teas.
