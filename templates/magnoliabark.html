Magnolia Bark (செண்பகப் பட்டை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Magnolia officinalis

TAMIL NAME: செண்பகப் பட்டை

PARTS USED: Bark

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Anti-Anxiety: Helps reduce stress and promotes calmness.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative damage.
  • Digestive Aid: Supports gut health and appetite.
  • Respiratory Support: Soothes coughs and phlegm.
  • Sleep Promoter: Helps improve sleep quality.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Stress & Anxiety

  • Benefit: Promotes relaxation and reduces nervous tension.
  • Application: Bark extract capsules or tea.

2. Digestive Issues

  • Benefit: Improves digestion and appetite.
  • Application: Decoction or tincture of the bark.

3. Respiratory Health

  • Benefit: Relieves cough and clears phlegm.
  • Application: Herbal tea or extract.

4. Sleep Improvement

  • Benefit: Promotes deeper and restful sleep.
  • Application: Taken as a tea before bedtime.

5. Anti-inflammatory & Antioxidant Support

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and oxidative stress.
  • Application: Bark extract or capsules as recommended.
