Valerian Root (தகரை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Valeriana officinalis

TAMIL NAME: வேலேரியன் வேர்

PARTS USED: Roots, Rhizomes

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Calming & Sedative: Helps induce sleep and reduce anxiety.
  • Anti-stress: Supports nervous system and reduces tension.
  • Muscle Relaxant: Relieves muscle cramps and spasms.
  • Digestive Aid: Supports healthy digestion.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces mild inflammation and discomfort.
  • Supports Mental Health: Enhances relaxation and focus.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Sleep Disorders & Insomnia

  • Benefit: Promotes restful sleep and reduces insomnia.
  • Application: Valerian root tea, tincture, or capsules before bedtime.

2. Anxiety & Stress

  • Benefit: Calms nervous system and reduces anxiety symptoms.
  • Application: Valerian tea or standardized root extract daily.

3. Muscle Cramps & Spasms

  • Benefit: Relieves cramps and tightness in muscles.
  • Application: Root extract capsules or warm infusion.

4. Mild Inflammation & Pain

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation-related discomfort.
  • Application: Valerian root poultice or oral extract.

5. Nervous System Support

  • Benefit: Supports relaxation and mental clarity.
  • Application: Daily intake as tea or supplement.
