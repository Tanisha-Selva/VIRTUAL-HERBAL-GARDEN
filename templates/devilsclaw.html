Devil’s Claw (சூர்நகம் )

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Harpagophytum procumbens

TAMIL NAME: டெவில்ஸ் கிளா (பயன்படும் ஆங்கில மூலிகை)

PARTS USED: Tuber, Roots

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Anti-inflammatory: Highly effective for joint swelling.
  • Pain-relieving: Reduces chronic muscle & bone pain.
  • Anti-rheumatic: Used for arthritis & joint stiffness.
  • Digestive stimulant: Improves appetite & digestion.
  • Detoxifying: Cleanses blood & supports metabolism.
  • Antioxidant: Protects tissues from degeneration.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Arthritis & Joint Pain (வாத வலி, மூட்டு வலி)

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation & joint stiffness.
  • Application: Root extract capsules or decoction.

2. Back Pain & Sciatica

  • Benefit: Provides long-term pain relief.
  • Application: Standardized Devil’s Claw supplements.

3. Muscle Cramps & Body Pain

  • Benefit: Relaxes muscles and reduces spasms.
  • Application: Taken as tea or powder with warm water.

4. Loss of Appetite & Poor Digestion

  • Benefit: Stimulates digestive enzymes.
  • Application: Mild infusion before meals.

5. Gout & Inflammatory Conditions

  • Benefit: Controls uric acid–related swelling.
  • Application: Root extract or herbal preparations.
