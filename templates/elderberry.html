Elderberry (மணத்தக்காளி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Sambucus nigra

TAMIL NAME: மணத்தக்காளி (மேற்கு மருத்துவ மூலிகை)

PARTS USED: Berries, Flowers

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Anti-viral: Strong action against cold & flu viruses.
  • Immuno-boosting: Enhances body’s natural defense.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces respiratory inflammation.
  • Antioxidant: Rich in anthocyanins & flavonoids.
  • Expectorant: Helps clear mucus & congestion.
  • Detoxifying: Cleanses the body & supports metabolism.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Cold & Flu (சளி, காய்ச்சல்)

  • Benefit: Reduces symptoms & speeds recovery.
  • Application: Elderberry syrup or tea.

2. Low Immunity (எதிர்ப்புச் சக்தி குறைவு)

  • Benefit: Strengthens immune system naturally.
  • Application: Daily extract or syrup.

3. Sore Throat & Respiratory Infection

  • Benefit: Reduces mucus, swelling & throat irritation.
  • Application: Warm elderflower tea or syrup.

4. Sinusitis & Congestion

  • Benefit: Clears nasal passages.
  • Application: Hot elderberry tea.

5. Antioxidant Protection

  • Benefit: Protects cells & reduces inflammation.
  • Application: Juice, supplements, capsules.
