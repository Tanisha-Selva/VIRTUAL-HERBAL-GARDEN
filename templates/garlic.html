PARTS USED: Bulbs, Leaves, Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Natural antibiotic and immune booster.
  • Cardiovascular Support: Reduces blood pressure and cholesterol.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and infections.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative damage.
  • Antimicrobial: Fights bacteria, viruses, and fungi.
  • Digestive Aid: Supports gut health and appetite.

4. Anti-inflammatory & Pain Relief
