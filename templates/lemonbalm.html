Lemon Balm (எலுமிச்சை தைலம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Melissa officinalis

TAMIL NAME: நெல்லிக்காய் தாவரம்

PARTS USED: Leaves, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Calming and mood-lifting herb.
  • Anti-anxiety: Reduces stress and nervous tension.
  • Digestive aid: Relieves bloating and indigestion.
  • Sleep aid: Promotes restful sleep.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Anti-viral: Supports immunity against minor infections.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Anxiety & Stress Relief

  • Benefit: Calms the mind and reduces tension.
  • Application: Lemon balm tea or leaf infusion.

2. Sleep Disorders & Insomnia

  • Benefit: Promotes restful sleep.
  • Application: Tea before bedtime or essential oil inhalation.

3. Digestive Discomfort

  • Benefit: Reduces bloating and aids digestion.
  • Application: Tea or decoction after meals.

4. Cold & Flu Support

  • Benefit: Helps fight minor viral infections.
  • Application: Lemon balm tea or combined with other herbal infusions.

5. Mood Enhancement

  • Benefit: Lifts mood and reduces nervous fatigue.
  • Application: Leaf infusion or aromatic oil inhalation.
