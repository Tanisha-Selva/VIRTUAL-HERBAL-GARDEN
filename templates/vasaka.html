Vasaka (ஆடாதோடை/ அஃதாடோடா வாசிகா)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Adhatoda vasica

TAMIL NAME: மலாபர் நட்டு / அஃதாடோடா வாசிகா

PARTS USED: Leaves, Roots, Flowers

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Expectorant: Helps in clearing respiratory tract and mucus.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation in lungs and throat.
  • Antimicrobial: Protects against bacterial and viral infections.
  • Respiratory Support: Supports healthy lungs and eases cough.
  • Fever Reduction: Mildly reduces fever.
  • Blood Purifying: Supports general detoxification.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Cough, Bronchitis & Asthma

  • Benefit: Reduces mucus, eases breathing, and calms cough.
  • Application: Leaf decoction, juice, or standardized extract.

2. Respiratory Infections

  • Benefit: Prevents and reduces severity of infections.
  • Application: Herbal tea or syrup preparation.

3. Fever & Common Cold

  • Benefit: Reduces mild fever and soothes throat irritation.
  • Application: Leaf infusion or warm decoction with honey.

4. Lung Health & Detoxification

  • Benefit: Supports healthy lung function and detox.
  • Application: Daily herbal tea or juice of leaves.

5. Anti-inflammatory & Immunity Support

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and boosts immunity.
  • Application: Taken as capsules, tincture, or decoction.
