Black Seed (கருஞ்சீரகம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Nigella sativa

TAMIL NAME: கருஞ்சீரகம் (Black Seed / Kalonji)

PARTS USED: Seeds, Seed oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful immune booster and natural detoxifier.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and joint pain.
  • Respiratory Support: Helps in asthma, cold, and sinus issues.
  • Digestive Aid: Improves appetite, reduces bloating.
  • Antioxidant Rich: Protects body from toxic damage.
  • Blood Sugar Support: Used traditionally for diabetes control.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Asthma & Respiratory Problems

  • Cure/Relief: Opens airways, reduces wheezing and congestion.
  • Application: Seed oil taken with warm water daily.

2. Diabetes & Blood Sugar Regulation

  • Cure/Relief: Helps balance glucose and improves insulin sensitivity.
  • Application: Powdered seeds taken before food.

3. Digestive Disorders

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces gas, bloating, and indigestion.
  • Application: Roasted seeds consumed after meals.

4. Joint Pain & Arthritis

  • Cure/Relief: Anti-inflammatory action reduces pain.
  • Application: Black seed oil massage on joints + internal use.

5. Skin Diseases & Hair Fall

  • Cure/Relief: Heals acne, eczema, fungal infections; promotes hair growth.
  • Application: Oil applied externally or consumed internally.
