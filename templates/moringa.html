Moringa (முருங்கை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Moringa oleifera

TAMIL NAME: முருங்கை

PARTS USED: Leaves, Seeds, Pods, Roots

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and joint pain.
  • Immune Booster: Strengthens overall immunity.
  • Supports Digestion: Improves gut health and appetite.
  • Blood Sugar Regulation: Helps maintain healthy glucose levels.
  • Cardiovascular Support: Promotes heart health and circulation.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Malnutrition & General Health

  • Benefit: Provides essential nutrients and supports growth.
  • Application: Fresh leaves or powdered leaf in food.

2. Diabetes & Blood Sugar Control

  • Benefit: Helps regulate blood sugar naturally.
  • Application: Leaf juice or powdered leaves with water.

3. Inflammation & Joint Pain

  • Benefit: Reduces swelling and pain in joints.
  • Application: Leaf extract or powder consumption.

4. Immune & Liver Support

  • Benefit: Strengthens immunity and detoxifies liver.
  • Application: Moringa tea, juice, or extract daily.

5. Heart Health & Blood Pressure

  • Benefit: Supports cardiovascular health and lowers hypertension.
  • Application: Regular use of leaves or seeds in diet.
