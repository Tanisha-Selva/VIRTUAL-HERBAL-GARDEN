Bhringraj (கரிசிலாங்கண்ணி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Eclipta alba / Eclipta prostrata

TAMIL NAME: கரிஞ்சி, மொன்நொக்கு, கறுமுருங்கை

PARTS USED: Leaves, Whole plant, Juice (Swarasa)

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Famous hair growth and liver-strengthening herb in Siddha medicine.
  • Kesariyam: Promotes hair growth and prevents hair fall.
  • Liver Tonic: Detoxifies and rejuvenates liver cells.
  • Cooling Herb: Reduces body heat and inflammation.
  • Anti-microbial: Fights infections and skin disorders.
  • Blood Purifier: Improves complexion and skin glow.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Hair Loss & Scalp Problems

  • Cure/Relief: Stimulates hair growth, prevents dandruff, stops premature greying.
  • Application: Bhringraj oil applied to scalp regularly.

2. Liver Disorders

  • Cure/Relief: Heals fatty liver, jaundice, and boosts liver functions.
  • Application: Fresh leaf juice or decoction consumed daily.

3. Skin Diseases & Psoriasis

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces itching, redness, and inflammation.
  • Application: Paste applied externally + internal decoction.

4. Body Heat & Ulcers

  • Cure/Relief: Coolant herb that reduces ulcers and acidity.
  • Application: Whole plant juice taken on empty stomach.

5. Eye Problems (Traditional Use)

  • Cure/Relief: Improves eyesight, reduces redness.
  • Application: Leaf juice used in specific Siddha formulations.

AS DESCRIBED IN SIDDHA LITERATURE

** Agathiyar Gunavagadam / Theraiyar Yamaga Venba / Siddha Materia Medica **
