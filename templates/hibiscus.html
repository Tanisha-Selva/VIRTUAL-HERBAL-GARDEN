Hibiscus (செம்பருத்தி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

TAMIL NAME: செம்பருத்தி

PARTS USED: Flowers, Leaves

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Hair Growth: Stimulates hair roots and reduces hair fall.
  • Cooling Effect: Reduces body heat and calms inflammation.
  • Skin Healing: Treats rashes, pimples, and irritation.
  • Antioxidant: Rich in Vitamin C and protects body cells.
  • Blood Purification: Helps detoxify blood naturally.
  • Menstrual Support: Helps regulate menstrual cycle.
