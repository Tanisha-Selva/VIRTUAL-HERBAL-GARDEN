Willow Bark (ஆற்றுப்பாலை )

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Salix alba

TAMIL NAME: வில்லோ மரத் தோல்

PARTS USED: Bark

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Analgesic: Naturally relieves pain, especially joint and muscle pain.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and inflammation.
  • Fever Reducer: Helps lower mild fevers.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative damage.
  • Supports Digestion: Helps ease minor digestive discomfort.
  • Blood Circulation: Improves overall circulation and joint health.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Arthritis & Joint Pain

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and pain in joints.
  • Application: Willow bark extract or powdered bark tea.

2. Headache & Muscle Pain

  • Benefit: Relieves headache and general body aches.
  • Application: Decoction or capsules of willow bark.

3. Fever & Common Cold

  • Benefit: Helps reduce mild fevers and discomfort.
  • Application: Brewed bark tea or infusion.

4. Inflammation & Swelling

  • Benefit: Reduces localized swelling and joint stiffness.
  • Application: Topical compress or oral decoction.

5. General Wellness & Blood Support

  • Benefit: Supports overall body health and circulation.
  • Application: Taken as herbal tea or standardized extract.
