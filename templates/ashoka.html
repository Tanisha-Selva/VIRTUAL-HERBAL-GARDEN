Ashoka (அசோக மரம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Saraca asoca

TAMIL NAME: அசோக மரம் (Asoka Maram)

PARTS USED: Bark, Flowers, Leaves

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Ashoka is renowned in Siddha medicine as a powerful women’s health tonic.
  • Stree Roga Nivarani: Treats menstrual disorders and strengthens the uterus.
  • Raktha Sthambhanam: Controls excessive bleeding.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain in reproductive organs.
  • Blood Purifier: Improves complexion and skin health.
  • Uterine Strengthener: Promotes healthy reproductive function.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Menstrual Disorders (Irregular Periods)

  • Cure/Relief: Normalizes cycle, reduces cramps, balances hormones.
  • Application: Ashoka bark decoction taken daily during treatment.

2. Excessive Bleeding (Menorrhagia)

  • Cure/Relief: Controls heavy flow and strengthens uterine tissues.
  • Application: Ashoka churna (powder) or Kashayam used under supervision.

3. Leucorrhoea (White Discharge)

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces infection, normalizes vaginal health.
  • Application: Bark decoction or tablet form prescribed in Siddha medicine.

4. Uterine Weakness

  • Cure/Relief: Strengthens uterus and improves fertility.
  • Application: Regular consumption of Ashoka arishtam or extract.

5. Skin Disorders & Blood Impurities

  • Cure/Relief: Clears acne, improves glow, detoxifies blood.
  • Application: Flower paste or decoction used internally and externally.

AS DESCRIBED IN SIDDHA LITERATURE

** Agathiyar Gunavagadam / Theraiyar Yamaga Venba **
