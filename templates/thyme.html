Thyme (ஓமம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Thymus vulgaris

TAMIL NAME: தைம்

PARTS USED: Leaves, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Antimicrobial: Fights harmful bacteria and viruses.
  • Digestive Aid: Promotes healthy digestion and reduces bloating.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation in the body.
  • Expectorant: Helps relieve cough and respiratory congestion.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Immune Support: Strengthens overall immunity.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Respiratory Health

  • Benefit: Relieves cough, bronchitis, and congestion.
  • Application: Thyme tea or infusion with honey.

2. Digestive Disorders

  • Benefit: Reduces bloating, indigestion, and gas.
  • Application: Consumed as herbal tea or added to meals.

3. Anti-inflammatory & Pain Relief

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and muscle pain.
  • Application: Thyme oil or infusion applied topically or ingested.

4. Immune Boost & Antioxidant Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells and strengthens immunity.
  • Application: Regular consumption of thyme tea or tincture.

5. Antimicrobial Uses

  • Benefit: Helps fight infections and maintain overall health.
  • Application: Thyme extract or essential oil in small doses.
