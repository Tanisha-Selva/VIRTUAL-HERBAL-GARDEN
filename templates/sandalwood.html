Sandalwood (சந்தனம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Santalum album

TAMIL NAME: சந்தனம்

PARTS USED: Wood, Oil, Powder

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Natural cooling and soothing agent for skin and body.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces redness, swelling, and irritation.
  • Antimicrobial: Protects against bacterial and fungal infections.
  • Relaxant: Calms the mind and promotes sleep.
  • Detoxifying: Supports skin health and purification.
  • Aromatherapeutic: Enhances mood and relieves stress.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Skin Health & Irritations

  • Benefit: Soothes inflammation, acne, and rashes.
  • Application: Sandalwood paste or oil applied topically.

2. Relaxation & Stress Relief

  • Benefit: Calms the mind and reduces anxiety.
  • Application: Sandalwood essential oil in aromatherapy or bath.

3. Anti-inflammatory Support

  • Benefit: Reduces swelling and irritation.
  • Application: Paste, powder, or oil on affected areas.

4. Skin Detox & Glow

  • Benefit: Promotes clear and healthy skin.
  • Application: Sandalwood mask or combined herbal formulations.

5. Aromatherapy & Mental Wellbeing

  • Benefit: Enhances mood, focus, and relaxation.
  • Application: Sandalwood oil diffused in room or massage.
