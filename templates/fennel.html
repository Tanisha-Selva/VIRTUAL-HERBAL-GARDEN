Fennel (சோம்பு)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Foeniculum vulgare

TAMIL NAME: சோம்பு/ பெருஞ்சீரகம்

PARTS USED: Seeds, Leaves, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Supports digestion and relieves bloating.
  • Carminative: Reduces gas and stomach discomfort.
  • Galactagogue: Promotes milk production in nursing mothers.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and mild pain.
  • Respiratory Aid: Clears congestion and supports lung health.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Digestive Disorders (Indigestion, Gas)

  • Benefit: Reduces bloating and improves appetite.
  • Application: Fennel tea or roasted seeds after meals.

2. Lactation Support

  • Benefit: Increases breast milk production.
  • Application: Daily intake of fennel seeds or tea.

3. Respiratory Health

  • Benefit: Clears congestion and supports lung function.
  • Application: Fennel seed decoction or inhalation of fennel steam.

4. Menstrual Health

  • Benefit: Helps reduce menstrual cramps and discomfort.
  • Application: Fennel tea or powder regularly during periods.

5. Antioxidant & General Health

  • Benefit: Protects body cells and supports immunity.
  • Application: Seeds, tea, or oil as part of diet.
