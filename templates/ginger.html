Ginger (இஞ்சி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Zingiber officinale

TAMIL NAME: இஞ்சி

PARTS USED: Rhizome (Root), Extracts, Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful digestive stimulant and carminative.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces joint and muscle pain.
  • Antiemetic: Relieves nausea and vomiting.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Warming Herb: Improves blood circulation and energy.
  • Immune Booster: Helps fight infections and colds.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Indigestion & Nausea

  • Benefit: Improves digestion and relieves stomach upset.
  • Application: Fresh ginger tea or decoction after meals.

2. Cold, Cough & Flu

  • Benefit: Reduces congestion and boosts immunity.
  • Application: Ginger tea with honey and lemon.

3. Joint Pain & Inflammation

  • Benefit: Relieves swelling and pain in muscles and joints.
  • Application: Ginger paste or oil applied topically, or decoction internally.

4. Respiratory Health

  • Benefit: Helps clear airways and reduce phlegm.
  • Application: Inhalation of ginger steam or ginger tea.

5. Nausea in Pregnancy & Motion Sickness

  • Benefit: Reduces morning sickness and travel sickness.
  • Application: Ginger candies, tea, or small fresh pieces chewed.
