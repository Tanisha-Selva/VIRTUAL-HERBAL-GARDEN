Chasteberry (அகத்தி மூலிகை / வைடெக்ஸ்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Vitex agnus-castus

TAMIL NAME: வைடெக்ஸ் மரம் / குன்றிமணி போன்ற மூலிகை / அகத்தி வகை

PARTS USED: Berries (Seeds), Leaves, Flowers

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Excellent hormone-balancing herb for women.
  • Regulates Menstrual Cycle: Naturally balances estrogen & progesterone.
  • Reduces PMS: Helpful for mood swings, irritability, breast tenderness.
  • Improves Fertility: Supports ovulation and reproductive health.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Useful in reducing swelling.
  • Mild Sedative: Reduces stress and anxiety.
