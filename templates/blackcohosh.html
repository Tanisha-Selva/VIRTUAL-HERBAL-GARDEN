Black Cohosh (கறுப்பு கோஹொஷ்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Actaea racemosa

TAMIL NAME: கறுப்பு கோஹொஷ்

PARTS USED: Root, Rhizome

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Supports female reproductive and hormonal balance.
  • Menopause Relief: Reduces hot flashes and mood swings.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Helps reduce joint and muscle pain.
  • Nervine Tonic: Calms the nervous system and reduces anxiety.
  • Uterine Support: Used traditionally for menstrual discomfort.

  • Application: Daily intake of standardized extract.

4. Anxiety & Stress Relief

  • Application: Tea or capsules taken in the evening.

5. Hormonal Imbalances

  • Application: Used under expert guidance as root tincture.
