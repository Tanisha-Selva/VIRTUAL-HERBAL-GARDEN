Chamomile (சீமைச்சாமந்தி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Matricaria chamomilla (German Chamomile) / Chamaemelum nobile (Roman Chamomile)

TAMIL NAME: கேமோமைல் பூ / சாமந்தி போன்ற மூலிகை

PARTS USED: Flowers

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Excellent calming and sleep-inducing herb.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain.
  • Digestive Soother: Relieves gas and indigestion.
  • Stress Relief: Eases anxiety and nervous tension.
  • Anti-spasmodic: Relaxes muscles and cramps.
  • Skin Healing: Used for wounds, rashes, and irritation.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Insomnia & Anxiety

  • Cure/Relief: Promotes relaxation and deeper sleep.
  • Application: Chamomile tea before bedtime.

2. Digestive Issues (Gas, Bloating, Indigestion)

  • Cure/Relief: Eases stomach discomfort.
  • Application: Warm tea after meals.

3. Skin Irritation & Wounds

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces redness, itchiness, and helps healing.
  • Application: Compress or wash made from flower decoction.

4. Menstrual Pain & Muscle Cramps

  • Cure/Relief: Relaxes muscles and reduces spasms.
  • Application: Tea consumed twice daily.

5. Cold, Sore Throat & Fever

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces inflammation, clears mucus.
  • Application: Inhalation of steam or tea consumption.
