Red Clover (தழுதலை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Trifolium pratense

TAMIL NAME: செம்மஞ்சள் மஞ்சள்

PARTS USED: Flowers, Leaves, Extracts

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Hormonal Support: Helps balance estrogen levels in women.
  • Blood Purifier: Promotes detoxification and supports liver function.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and swelling.
  • Rich in Nutrients: Contains vitamins, minerals, and isoflavones.
  • Supports Respiratory Health: Helps with coughs and lung conditions.
  • Antioxidant: Protects against oxidative stress.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Menopause & Hormonal Balance

  • Benefit: Eases hot flashes and hormonal fluctuations.
  • Application: Red clover tea or standardized extracts daily.

2. Blood Purification & Liver Support

  • Benefit: Detoxifies blood and supports liver function.
  • Application: Herbal infusion from flowers and leaves.

3. Respiratory Health

  • Benefit: Helps relieve cough and congestion.
  • Application: Tea or decoction from dried flowers.

4. Skin & Anti-inflammatory Support

  • Benefit: Reduces skin inflammation and supports skin health.
  • Application: Topical poultices or herbal tea internally.

5. General Health & Immunity

  • Benefit: Rich nutrients and antioxidants support overall well-being.
  • Application: Regular consumption of tea or powdered extracts.
