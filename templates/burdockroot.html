Burdock Root (கழுத்தான் கிழங்கு)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Arctium lappa

TAMIL NAME: கழுத்தான் கிழங்கு (Kazhuthan Kizhangu)

PARTS USED: Root, Seeds, Leaves

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Powerful blood purifier and detoxifying herb.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and pain.
  • Digestive Tonic: Enhances digestion and appetite.
  • Liver Cleanser: Supports bile flow and detoxification.
  • Anti-bacterial: Useful for skin infections.
  • Diuretic: Removes excess fluids and toxins.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Skin Diseases (Acne, Eczema, Psoriasis)

  • Cure/Relief: Clears toxins, reduces itching and inflammation.
  • Application: Decoction consumed daily; paste applied externally.

2. Liver Disorders & Detox

  • Cure/Relief: Cleanses the liver and purifies blood.
  • Application: Root decoction or extract taken regularly.

3. Digestive Problems

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces gas, constipation, and indigestion.
  • Application: Warm tea before meals.

4. Joint Pain & Arthritis

  • Cure/Relief: Decreases pain, stiffness, and inflammation.
  • Application: Root powder taken with warm water.

5. Kidney & Urinary Disorders

  • Cure/Relief: Acts as a natural diuretic, flushing toxins.
  • Application: Decoction consumed twice a day.
