Green Tea (கிரீன் டீ)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Camellia sinensis

TAMIL NAME: கிரீன் டீ

PARTS USED: Leaves (Fresh or Dried), Extracts

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Rich in antioxidants and polyphenols.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces body inflammation and oxidative stress.
  • Boosts Metabolism: Supports weight management.
  • Improves Mental Alertness: Contains caffeine and L-theanine.
  • Supports Heart Health: Helps reduce cholesterol and blood pressure.
  • Immune Support: Strengthens the body’s natural defense.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Weight Management & Metabolism

  • Benefit: Supports fat oxidation and boosts metabolism.
  • Application: Daily consumption of brewed green tea.

2. Antioxidant & Detox Support

  • Benefit: Protects cells from damage and supports liver detox.
  • Application: Green tea leaves or extract regularly.

3. Heart & Cholesterol Health

  • Benefit: Reduces bad cholesterol and promotes cardiovascular health.
  • Application: 2–3 cups of brewed green tea daily.

4. Mental Alertness & Focus

  • Benefit: Enhances concentration and cognitive function.
  • Application: Consume brewed green tea in the morning or afternoon.

5. Immunity & General Health

  • Benefit: Supports immune system and overall well-being.
  • Application: Regular intake of green tea or its extracts.
