Lavender (லாவெண்டர்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Lavandula angustifolia

TAMIL NAME: லாவெண்டர்

PARTS USED: Flowers, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Natural relaxant and calming herb.
  • Sleep Aid: Helps relieve insomnia and promotes restful sleep.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces mild pain and swelling.
  • Antimicrobial: Protects against minor infections.
  • Digestive Support: Reduces bloating and discomfort.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Stress & Anxiety Relief

  • Benefit: Calms the mind and reduces nervous tension.
  • Application: Lavender tea or inhalation of essential oil.

2. Sleep Disorders & Insomnia

  • Benefit: Promotes restful and deep sleep.
  • Application: Essential oil in diffuser or pillow spray.

3. Minor Pain & Inflammation

  • Benefit: Relieves mild joint or muscle discomfort.
  • Application: Topical lavender oil or poultice.

4. Skin Health & Wounds

  • Benefit: Promotes healing of minor cuts, burns, or skin irritation.
  • Application: Diluted lavender oil applied externally.

5. Digestive Support

  • Benefit: Reduces bloating and stomach discomfort.
  • Application: Lavender tea after meals.
