Boswellia (குங்கிலியம்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Boswellia serrata

TAMIL NAME: குங்கிலியம்

PARTS USED: Resin (Gum), Bark

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Strong anti-inflammatory agent.
  • Analgesic: Relieves chronic joint and muscle pain.
  • Expectorant: Improves lung capacity and eases breathing.
  • Digestive Tonic: Heals gut inflammation.
  • Antioxidant: Removes toxins from the body.
  • Blood purifier: Supports skin & immunity.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Arthritis & Joint Pain

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces pain, swelling, stiffness.
  • Application: Resin powder or tablets taken daily.

2. Asthma & Breathing Issues

  • Cure/Relief: Clears airways, reduces wheezing.
  • Application: Resin decoction consumed.

3. Inflammatory Bowel Diseases (IBD, IBS)

  • Cure/Relief: Heals stomach lining, reduces bloating.
  • Application: Powder taken with warm water.

4. Skin Diseases & Wound Healing

  • Cure/Relief: Reduces infection, itching.
  • Application: Paste applied externally.

5. Stress & Mood Disorders

  • Cure/Relief: Calms mind, reduces anxiety.
  • Application: Resin fumes traditionally inhaled.

AS DESCRIBED IN SIDDHA LITERATURE

** Agathiyar Gunavagadam / Siddha Materia Medica / Theraiyar Maruthuvam **
