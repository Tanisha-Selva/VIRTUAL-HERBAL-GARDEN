Mullein (நாட்டுவஞ்சி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Verbascum thapsus

TAMIL NAME: நாட்டுவஞ்சி

PARTS USED: Leaves, Flowers, Roots

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Respiratory Support: Soothes lungs and reduces cough.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and irritation.
  • Antimicrobial: Fights respiratory infections.
  • Diuretic: Supports urinary tract health.
  • Wound Healing: Promotes skin repair and reduces inflammation.
  • Relaxant: Calms respiratory tract and mild muscle tension.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Cough & Bronchitis

  • Benefit: Reduces cough and clears mucus.
  • Application: Mullein tea or syrup daily.

2. Respiratory Infections

  • Benefit: Antimicrobial effect fights bacteria and viruses.
  • Application: Flower or leaf decoction.

3. Inflammation & Pain Relief

  • Benefit: Reduces swelling and irritation.
  • Application: Poultice of leaves on affected area.

4. Urinary Health

  • Benefit: Diuretic effect supports kidney and bladder function.
  • Application: Mullein tea or infusion.

5. Skin & Wound Healing

  • Benefit: Promotes faster healing of minor cuts and burns.
  • Application: Leaf poultice or infused oil topically.
