Dong Quai (டாங் குவாய்)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Angelica sinensis

TAMIL NAME: டாங் குவாய் / பெண் ஜின்செங்க் (Traditional Chinese herb)

PARTS USED: Root

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Women's Health Tonic: Strengthens reproductive system.
  • Blood Purifier: Improves blood flow & nourishes tissues.
  • Hormone Balancer: Supports menstrual cycle regularity.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces pelvic & muscle inflammation.
  • Mild Sedative: Relaxes nerves & reduces stress.
  • Restorative: Boosts strength, vitality & overall energy.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Menstrual Disorders (Irregular Periods, Cramps)

  • Benefit: Regulates cycle & reduces pain.
  • Application: Decoction, capsules, or powdered root.

2. Menopause Symptoms

  • Benefit: Reduces hot flashes, mood swings & dryness.
  • Application: Standardized Dong Quai supplements.

3. Anemia & Low Blood Levels

  • Benefit: Nourishes blood & increases hemoglobin.
  • Application: Boiled root tonic.

4. Pelvic Pain & Endometriosis

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation & pain.
  • Application: Decoction taken warm.

5. Weakness, Fatigue & Low Vitality

  • Benefit: Strengthens the body & restores energy.
  • Application: Powdered root with warm water or honey.
