Licorice Root (முளகுசேனை வேர்க்கடலை)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Glycyrrhiza glabra

TAMIL NAME: முளகுசேனை வேர்க்கடலை

PARTS USED: Roots, Extracts

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Soothing herb for respiratory and digestive systems.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation in throat and stomach.
  • Expectorant: Clears mucus from respiratory tract.
  • Digestive aid: Helps in ulcers and gastritis.
  • Immune booster: Strengthens the body against infections.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Cough & Sore Throat

  • Benefit: Soothes throat irritation and reduces coughing.
  • Application: Licorice root decoction or syrup.

2. Gastric Ulcers & Indigestion

  • Benefit: Protects stomach lining and improves digestion.
  • Application: Root extract or powder with warm water.

3. Anti-inflammatory & Immune Support

  • Benefit: Reduces inflammation and strengthens immunity.
  • Application: Regular consumption of licorice decoction.

4. Respiratory Congestion

  • Benefit: Clears phlegm and eases breathing.
  • Application: Licorice tea or combined with herbal remedies.

5. Liver & Detox Support

  • Benefit: Supports liver function and detoxification.
  • Application: Root extract or herbal formulations.
