Hops (முசுக்கட்டையினச் செடி)

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Humulus lupulus

TAMIL NAME: முசுக்கட்டையினச் செடி

PARTS USED: Flower Cones, Extracts, Essential Oil

KEY TRADITIONAL PROPERTIES

  • Natural sedative and calming herb.
  • Sleep Aid: Helps relieve insomnia and promotes restful sleep.
  • Digestive Support: Reduces bloating and improves digestion.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Helps reduce swelling and mild pain.
  • Antioxidant: Protects cells from oxidative stress.
  • Menstrual Support: Helps ease mild menstrual discomfort.

TRADITIONAL USES, DISEASES CURED, & APPLICATION

1. Sleep Disorders & Insomnia

  • Benefit: Calms the mind and induces sleep.
  • Application: Hops tea or tincture before bedtime.

2. Anxiety & Stress Relief

  • Benefit: Reduces nervous tension and mild anxiety.
  • Application: Hops infusion or herbal preparations.

3. Digestive Support

  • Benefit: Improves appetite and reduces bloating.
  • Application: Hops decoction or tea after meals.

4. Mild Pain & Inflammation

  • Benefit: Reduces mild aches and joint inflammation.
  • Application: Hops poultice or extract as recommended.

5. Menstrual Discomfort

  • Benefit: Eases mild cramps and discomfort.
  • Application: Hops tea or combined herbal formulations.
